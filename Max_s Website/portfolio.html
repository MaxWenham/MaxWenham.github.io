Videography

Videography is one of my main passions. I love cinematograhy, video editing, and overall just the whole process. I've been making films and videos since I was 10 years old. I started out by making comedy videos wioth my family and friends, and I've been making content ever since. I am always looking to improve my video making skills, every time I make a new video I think to myself "What can I do better?" I've been making videos for about 5 years now and I am still just as if not even more passinate about it.

Photography

Photography is one of my more recent hobbies. I got into it this year, and I've loved it. I take pictures of a lot of different things ranging from my other hobbies to random pictures of nature. Through my love for Photography I also found picture editing in programs such as photoshop and lightroom. I actually quite enjoy editing photos, almost more then taking them. I like the sense of creative freedom it gives me.
